# CHCC Website — Gemini Image Generation Guide

## How to Use
1. Open Google Gemini (paid)
2. Copy a prompt below
3. Paste the **Style Suffix** at the end of every prompt
4. Generate 4 variants, pick the best
5. Save files using the suggested filename and drop into `public/images/`

---

## Style Suffix — Add to EVERY Prompt

```
Photorealistic professional photography. Warm golden-hour lighting with soft saffron and amber tones. Natural, candid moment — not staged or stock-photo stiff. Shot on full-frame DSLR, 35mm lens, shallow depth of field. Color palette: warm saffron oranges, earth greens, cream and ivory backgrounds. People are Indian-American, multigenerational — children, adults, seniors together. Modern, welcoming, premium community center aesthetic. NOT a traditional temple setting. No text, no watermarks, no borders.
```

## Tips

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| Faces look generic | Add: *"South Indian and North Indian features represented, authentic Indian-American diaspora appearance"* |
| Too much religious imagery | Add: *"minimal religious iconography, secular community center aesthetic"* |
| Colors feel off | Add: *"warm saffron #F47C36 and earth green #4A7C59 color palette dominant"* |
| People look too posed | Add: *"candid documentary style, people unaware of camera"* |
| Background too busy | Add: *"clean simple background, subject in sharp focus"* |
