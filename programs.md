# CHCC Programs & Classes — Complete Guide

*This file provides the content for the "Programs" section of the website, including drop-down menus, program pages, and enrollment information.*

---

## Program Philosophy

Every CHCC program is designed around three outcomes:
1. **Identity** — Help participants connect with their Dharmic roots.
2. **Health** — Improve physical, mental, and spiritual wellbeing.
3. **Community** — Build lasting bonds across generations and backgrounds.

Programs are open to all — members receive discounted pricing; non-members are always welcome.

## 1. Early Education & Youth Programs

### Aum School (PreK – Grade 5)
*Building tomorrow's leaders on ancient wisdom*

**Age Group:** 3–11 years
**Schedule:** Full-day school, Monday–Friday, 7:30 AM – 5:30 PM (extended care available)
**Enrollment:** Open enrollment; waiting list priority for current CHCC members

**Curriculum Highlights:**
- **Vedic Mathematics:** Sutras for mental arithmetic, estimation, and number intuition — shown to improve math scores by 20–40% in peer studies.
- **Sanskrit Phonetics (Age 4+):** The world's most phonetically precise language; builds memory, focus, and linguistic intelligence.
- **Yoga & Pranayama (Daily, 20 min):** Embedded into the school day for emotional regulation and focus.
- **STEM + Dharma Projects:** Hands-on science aligned with ecological values (water conservation, solar energy, composting).
- **Character Education:** Yamas and Niyamas (ethical principles) woven into daily routines — ahimsa, satya, saucha.
- **Indian History & Geography:** Age-appropriate exploration of India's civilizations, arts, and contributions to science and philosophy.

**Enrichment Activities:**
- Weekly community garden time at the Gaushala
- Monthly field trips (art museums, science centers, civic institutions)
- Annual "Vidhyarambham" ceremony (traditional start-of-learning celebration for new students)
- Year-end "Convocation" with Sanskrit recitation and performance showcase

**Tuition:** Contact admissions for current rates; sliding-scale scholarships available.

## 2. Yoga & Wellness Programs

### Contemplative Yoga (All Levels)
*Authentic Hatha Yoga — not fitness, but transformation*

**Formats:**
- *Morning Sadhana:* 6:00 AM (Mon/Wed/Fri) — Surya Namaskar, Pranayama, meditation. 60 min.
- *Family Yoga:* Saturdays 9:00 AM — Parents and children practice together. 45 min.
- *Restorative Yoga:* Tuesdays/Thursdays 7:00 PM — Prop-supported, deeply relaxing. 75 min.
- *Online Yoga:* Live-streamed morning sessions; recordings available for members.

**Senior-Specific: "Sandhya Yoga" (Evening/Gentle)**
- Modified postures for arthritis, joint replacement, and balance challenges.
- Chair yoga options available.
- Community tea circle follows each class.
- Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays at 4:00 PM.

---

### Yoga Teacher Training (200-Hour Certification)
*Build a homegrown pipeline of authentic instructors*

**Format:** Weekend immersive (8 months) or 4-week intensive summer program
**Certification:** Yoga Alliance RYT-200 accredited
**Curriculum:**
- Hatha, Vinyasa, and Pranayama methodology
- Anatomy & physiology for yoga teachers
- Sanskrit terminology and chanting
- Yoga philosophy (Patanjali's Yoga Sutras, Bhagavad Gita)
- Teaching practicum (30+ hours of observed teaching)
- Business of yoga: how to build a sustainable teaching practice

**CHCC Commitment:** Graduates are offered teaching opportunities at CHCC, creating a self-sustaining instructor ecosystem.

## 3. Heritage Arts & Athletics

### Bharatanatyam Academy
*The divine language of classical dance*

**Director:** Seeking a senior Bharatanatyam acharya with Kalakshetra or Rukmini Devi lineage
**Levels:** Beginner (age 5+) → Intermediate → Advanced → Rangapravesh (debut performance)
**Schedule:** Tuesday/Thursday evenings + Saturday mornings
**Milestones:** Students progress toward the traditional Arangetram debut performance — a celebrated CHCC community event.

**Additional Dance Forms:**
- Kathak (Lucknow and Jaipur gharanas)
- Odissi
- Kuchipudi
- Mohiniyattam
- Bollywood Fitness (adult cardio dance; no classical experience required)
- Folk dances: Garba, Dandiya, Bhangra, Lavani (seasonal programs)

---

### Kalaripayattu Martial Arts
*The mother of all martial arts*

**Age Group:** 6 years and up
**Levels:**
- *Meythari (Body Training):* Foundational movements, flexibility, body control — Age 6–12
- *Kolthari (Weapon Training):* Stick combat — Age 12+
- *Angathari (Metal Weapons):* Advanced sword, shield, and spear — Teens and adults
- *Verumkai (Bare-Hand Combat):* Advanced strikes and vital point (marma) techniques

**Special Programs:**
- Women's Self-Defense: 6-week workshop using Kalaripayattu principles
- Performance Troupe: For cultural festivals and demonstrations

---

### Mallakhamba (Aerial Gymnastics)
*Ancient strength training reimagined*

**Age Group:** 8 years and up
**Equipment:** Traditional vertical teak poles (30 ft), rope Mallakhamba, hanging apparatus
**Schedule:** Saturdays 10 AM–12 PM; Tuesday/Thursday evenings 6–8 PM
**Benefits:** Exceptional core strength, grip, spatial awareness, and mental focus — proven in Maharashtra school systems.

**Competitive Track:** Prepare students for state and national Mallakhamba competitions; CHCC aims to host the Carolinas' first Mallakhamba invitational tournament.

### Music Academy

**Instruments Offered:**
- *Tabla:* India's premier percussion instrument; taught in Benares and Delhi gharana traditions
- *Sitar:* North Indian classical string instrument; Ravi Shankar and Nikhil Banerjee traditions
- *Veena:* Carnatic classical instrument; foundational for South Indian music
- *Mridangam:* Carnatic primary rhythm instrument
- *Harmonium:* For bhajan, kirtan, and North Indian classical support
- *Flute (Bansuri):* Hindustani classical style

**Vocal Programs:**
- Carnatic vocal (beginner through advanced)
- Hindustani vocal (beginner through intermediate)
- Bhajan & Kirtan ensemble (all levels; community practice every Friday evening)

**Annual Events:**
- *Swara Utsav:* Annual music festival showcasing CHCC student and invited guest artists
- *Guru Vandana:* Teacher appreciation concert on Guru Purnima

---

### Vedic Chanting Program
*Ancient sound technology for modern minds*

**Texts Covered:** Vedic Suktas, Upanishad passages, Vishnu Sahasranama, Lalita Sahasranama, Hanuman Chalisa
**Teaching Method:** Step-by-step phonetic training using Vedic pitch accents (udatta, anudatta, svarita)
**Research Evidence:** Regular Vedic chanting has been shown to increase gray matter density in memory-related brain regions (National Brain Research Centre study, India).
**Levels:**
- *Svadhyaya Level 1:* Phonetics and basic hymns (3 months)
- *Svadhyaya Level 2:* Vedic suktas and proper intonation (6 months)
- *Svadhyaya Level 3:* Full ceremony chanting and training to lead community prayers (1 year)

## 4. Senior Programs — "The Oasis"

*Honoring the wisdom of our elders*

### Discover Our Roots (Intergenerational Mentorship)
**Structure:** Retiring first-generation Indian professionals (engineers, doctors, professors) are paired with high school and college students for 6-month mentorship relationships.
**Format:** Monthly in-person meetings at CHCC + 2 virtual check-ins per month
**Outcomes:** Career guidance, cultural transmission, emotional fulfillment for seniors, professional direction for youth

### Vedanta Study Circle
**Schedule:** Every Sunday morning 9:30–11:00 AM
**Texts:** Bhagavad Gita (chapter-by-chapter), Upanishads, Vivekananda's complete works, Patanjali's Yoga Sutras
**Format:** Rotating discussion facilitators; open to all faiths and philosophical backgrounds
**Special Guests:** Quarterly visits from swamis, scholars, and interfaith religious leaders

### Senior Wellness Cluster
- *Contemplative Yoga:* Mon/Wed/Fri 4:00 PM (see Yoga section)
- *Memory Fitness Circle:* Vedic chanting, memory games, and Sanskrit word study (proven cognitive benefits)
- *Art Therapy:* Weekly rangoli, mandala drawing, and Indian folk art sessions
- *Health Talks:* Monthly presentations by Indian-American physicians on Ayurveda, nutrition, diabetes management, and mental health
- *Senior Social Fridays:* Carom, chess, antakshari, and cultural films — combating social isolation

### Senior Transportation & Outreach
- Volunteer-driven pickup service for members who cannot drive.
- Weekly welfare calls from trained CHCC volunteers.
- Festival meal delivery: Prasad delivered to homebound seniors during Diwali, Holi, and Navratri.

## Program Registration & Pricing Philosophy

- **CHCC Annual Membership:** $150/individual; $300/family — includes discounts on all programs, free access to select events, and priority registration.
- **Scholarship Fund:** No child is turned away for lack of funds. The CHCC Scholarship Fund covers 100% of program costs for qualifying families.
- **Community Classes:** Select programs (weekly yoga, Friday bhajan, Sewa events) are always free to all Charlotte residents — no membership required.
- **Corporate Wellness Partnerships:** Companies may sponsor employee memberships or program access as part of their wellness benefits.
