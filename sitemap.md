## 1. Homepage (`/`)

**Purpose:** First impression for all visitors — donors, prospective members, local press, and community partners. Must convey CHCC's vision, prove community viability, and prompt action within 10 seconds.

**SEO Title:** Charlotte Hindu Community Center | Heritage, Wellness & Community

**Sections (in order):**

### 1.1 Hero Section
- Full-viewport image/video background: golden-hour outdoor yoga or festival gathering
- Overlay text:
  - **Eyebrow:** "Charlotte, NC — Est. 2024"
  - **H1:** "Where Heritage Meets Harmony"
  - **Subhead:** "A home for every generation — rooted in Dharmic values, open to all."
- **CTAs (two):**
  - Primary: [Donate Now] — Saffron button

- Animated counter cards: Events Hosted | Attendees | Volunteer Hours | lbs Food Donated

### 1.6 Community in Action (Activity Report Preview)

### 1.7 Fundraising Campaign Banner

## Technical & SEO Requirements

### SEO Metadata (per page)
- Unique `<title>` tag: "[Page Name] | Charlotte Hindu Community Center"
- Meta description: 150–160 characters, unique per page, includes primary keyword
- Open Graph tags for Facebook/social sharing (image, title, description)
- Twitter Card tags
- JSON-LD structured data: Organization, Event (for events), LocalBusiness

### Target Keywords
- "Hindu community center Charlotte NC"
- "Indian community center Charlotte"
- "Aum school Charlotte"
- "yoga Charlotte NC"
- "Ayurveda Charlotte"
- "Bharatanatyam classes Charlotte"
- "Gaushala Charlotte"
- "Indian cultural events Charlotte"
- "Sanskrit classes Charlotte NC"
- "Hindu nonprofit Charlotte"

### Performance Requirements
- Lighthouse Performance score: ≥ 90 (mobile)
- Core Web Vitals: LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1
- Images: WebP format with lazy loading; AVIF for modern browsers
- Fonts: Preloaded; subset to characters used
- No render-blocking scripts

### Analytics
- Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — with cookie consent banner
- Google Tag Manager for flexible event tracking
- Track: Donation form starts, completions, tier selections, volunteer form submissions, newsletter signups, PDF downloads
- Facebook Pixel (for fundraising campaigns)
- Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity (for heatmaps and session recording)
